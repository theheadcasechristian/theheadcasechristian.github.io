About Me

You aren’t looking for a "perfect" Christian life. You’re looking for a true one. If you’ve ever felt like you’re "too much" for the church pews—too loud, too traumatized, too skeptical, or just too overwhelmed—you belong here.

I’m Erin, and I spent a long time trying to be the "perfect" daughter of a deacon and Sunday School teacher. I know the pressure of the pedestal. But life? Life had other plans. Between a teen pregnancy, 18 years in an abusive marriage, navigating chronic illness, and managing a beautifully chaotic ADHD brain, I learned that God doesn’t live on a pedestal. He lives in the depths.

Why This Blog Exists

I’m a 47-year-old mother, a Georgia Bulldogs fan (Go Dawgs!), a recovery program leader, and a woman who refuses to pay full price for anything. But more than that, I am a seeker of proof.

I don't believe the Bible is a collection of "nice stories." I believe it is a historical and scientific powerhouse that holds up under the microscope of reality. This isn’t a place for blind faith; it’s a place for informed, battle-tested belief. We talk about it all here:

  • The Evidence: How history and science back the Word.
  • The Struggle: Navigating PTSD, ADHD, divorce, and the exhaustion of the "sandwich generation".
  • The Relationships: How to love people when it's hard, and how to set boundaries when necessary.
  • The Aftermath: What happens to your life once you decide to follow Christ (spoiler: it’s not always sunshine, but it is always worth it).

My "Signature" is the Scars

I’ve been teaching the Word since I was 11 years old. I’ve led worship, run Bible studies, and worked in the trenches of recovery. But my greatest credentials aren't my titles—they are my scars.

I’ve walked through the valley of infertility, the sting of betrayal, and the daily grind of homeschooling and chronic pain. If I’ve learned anything, it’s the truth of Psalm 139:7-10:

"If I make my bed in the depths, you are there... even there your hand will guide me, your right hand will hold me fast."

Whether you are in the heavens or the "depths" right now, I want you to know you aren’t alone, and you aren’t "crazy."

A Few Things You Should Know About Me

  • I’m a Dog Whisperer: I can’t keep a goldfish alive to save my life, but I can speak "Canine" fluently.
  • I Know My Way Around a Garage: I might know more about your car than you do.
  • I’m Inked: Yes, I have tattoos. No, they don't make me any less of a daughter of the King.
  • I’m an ADHD Mind: This blog is a reflection of my brain—fast-moving, deeply curious, and covering a million topics because everything matters to God.

I don't expect every post to change your life, but I hope one does. I’ve written about everything from the science of the Resurrection to the reality of parenting a child with ADHD.
